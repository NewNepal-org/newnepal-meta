### Recommendations for पररच्छेद- ४: कायययोजनाको व्रिहत् तालिका, रणनीति २

#### On रणनीति २ (७) "भ्रष्टाचारसम्बधी सूचना तथा पुर्व सूचना दिने" and रणनीति २ (८) भ्रष्टाचारजधय कसूरको अनुसन्धानमा कृत्रिम वौध्दिकता (AI) ..." 

- Nepal is not ready for an AI detection system as digitization hasn't caught up.
- The 1-2 year implementation time periods are too short. Let's increase the time period to 5 years.

#### On रणनीति २ (15) "नेपालका स्थानीय तहसम्मका कायायलयहरूमा उच्च गतिको internet"

Change "नेपालका सिैस्थानमा इधटरनेटको व्यिस्था भएको हनुे।" to "Minimum 30 Mbps downlink internet speed availability at all government offices"

Also guarantee internet access for the public in government offices, at minimum "5 Mbps downlink."

#### On रणनीति २ (16) "सरकारी कर्मचारी, बैंक कर्मचारी, प्रविधि विज्ञ, ..."

Change indicator "तालिम मार्फत ..." to measurable figures: number of participants, number of programs, etc.

#### On रणनीति २ (17) "अख्तियार दुरुपयोग अनुसन्धान आयोग, ..."


Change "प्रभावकारी सूचना तथा समन्वय संयन्त्रको स्थापना भएको हनुे।".

If building a new tool, measure its adoption (e.g. number of messages per month), speed of delivery (how fast messages are delivered), and reliability (software availability and failure rate).

#### On रणनीति २ (२३) "भ्रष्टाचार विरुद्धको जनचेतना अभिृवृद्धिका लागि सूचना ..."

Change "सूचना शिछ्या तथा सञ्चार सामाग्रीको तयारी र वितरण भएको हनुे"

to something more measurable, including quality and quantity of distribution.

#### On रणनीति २ (२4)

Change "भ्रष्टाचार विरुद्ध सबै जिल्लामा नागरिक निगरानी संस्था (Citizen Jury) गठन भएको हुने।" to include pilot projects as one of the first milestones.

### New recommendations

### Increase corruption information accessibility
a. Guarantee internet availability of Nepal government websites at high bandwidths.
b. Digitize all publicly-accessible government documents with plans for disaster recovery.
c. Mandate all new press releases, reports, etc.  to be available online from official government websites within 24 hours.
d. Perform an independent accessibility / quality analysis of government websites and make the reports available to the public.
e. Make all publicly viewable court cases and documents available online, including orders, interim orders.

### Fund civic accountability initiatives
Nepal Government Fund should fund civic initiatives to improve the public accountability infrastructure, so that they can become less dependent on foreign governments and foreign interests.

### Fund university research/projects
Nepal Government should increase engagements with universities by funding anti-corruption research and training programs.
