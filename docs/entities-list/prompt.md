# AI Research Prompt: Comprehensive Nepal Government Office Index

## Objective
Create a comprehensive, verified index of ALL government offices in Nepal, from federal level down to municipality and ward level (approximately 6,743 wards across 753 local governments). This should be the most complete, accurate, and up-to-date directory of Nepal's government entities possible, with contact information, addresses, and organizational hierarchies.

## Research Requirements

### Scope
You are tasked with researching and documenting every government office, department, agency, and administrative unit in Nepal's federal democratic republic structure (established by the 2015 Constitution). This includes all entities across the three-tier government system:

1. **Federal Level Offices** (संघीय तह)
   - Office of the President (राष्ट्रपतिको कार्यालय) - Sheetal Niwas, Maharajgunj
   - Office of the Vice President (उपराष्ट्रपतिको कार्यालय) - Kantipath area
   - Office of the Prime Minister & Council of Ministers (प्रधानमन्त्री तथा मन्त्रिपरिषद् कार्यालय) - Singha Durbar
   - All 20 federal ministries and their subordinate departments
   - Constitutional bodies (Election Commission, CIAA, Auditor General, PSC, NHRC, etc.)
   - Federal agencies (Nepal Police, Armed Police Force, Nepal Army, NID)
   - Public enterprises (NEA, NOC, Nepal Telecom, Nepal Airlines, etc.)
   - Regulatory bodies and specialized agencies
   - Supreme Court and federal judicial offices

2. **Provincial Level Offices** (प्रदेशीय तह) - All 7 Provinces
   - **Koshi Province** (कोशी प्रदेश) - Capital: Biratnagar
   - **Madhesh Province** (मधेश प्रदेश) - Capital: Janakpur  
   - **Bagmati Province** (बागमती प्रदेश) - Capital: Hetauda
   - **Gandaki Province** (गण्डकी प्रदेश) - Capital: Pokhara
   - **Lumbini Province** (लुम्बिनी प्रदेश) - Capital: Deukhuri, Dang
   - **Karnali Province** (कर्णाली प्रदेश) - Capital: Birendranagar, Surkhet
   - **Sudurpashchim Province** (सुदूरपश्चिम प्रदेश) - Capital: Godawari, Kailali
   
   For each province:
   - Office of the Governor (प्रदेश प्रमुखको कार्यालय)
   - Office of Chief Minister & Council of Ministers
   - Provincial Assembly Secretariat (प्रदेश सभा सचिवालय)
   - Provincial ministries (typically 7-12 per province)
   - Provincial Public Service Commission
   - Provincial Attorney General's Office
   - High Courts and provincial judicial offices

3. **District Level Offices** (जिल्ला तह) - All 77 Districts
   - **District Administration Office (DAO)** (जिल्ला प्रशासन कार्यालय) - headed by Chief District Officer (CDO)
   - **District Coordination Committee (DCC)** (जिल्ला समन्वय समिति) - coordinates local governments
   - **District Court** (जिल्ला अदालत) - one in each district headquarters
   - **District Police Office (DPO)** (जिल्ला प्रहरी कार्यालय) - led by Superintendent of Police
   - **District Prison** (कारागार) - detention facilities
   - **District Postal Service Office** - mail and postal services
   - **Sectoral offices** (now mostly under provincial control):
     - District Health Office (स्वास्थ्य कार्यालय)
     - Education Development Coordination Units
     - Agriculture Knowledge Centers
     - Division Forest Offices
     - Land Revenue Offices (Malpot) - still federal
     - Survey Offices - still federal
     - Road Division Offices
     - District Government Attorney Office

4. **Local Level Offices** (स्थानीय तह) - All 753 Local Government Units
   - **6 Metropolitan Cities** (महानगरपालिका):
     - Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Biratnagar, Pokhara, Bharatpur, Birgunj
   - **11 Sub-Metropolitan Cities** (उपमहानगरपालिका):
     - Janakpur, Hetauda, Itahari, Dharan, Kalaiya, Jitpur Simara, Butwal, Nepalgunj, Ghorahi, Dhangadhi, Bhimdatta
   - **276 Municipalities** (नगरपालिका): Urban local governments
   - **460 Rural Municipalities** (गाउँपालिका): Rural local governments
   
   Each local government has:
   - Municipal/Rural Municipal Office (नगरपालिका/गाउँपालिका कार्यालय)
   - Mayor/Chairperson Office
   - Local sectoral offices and service centers

5. **Ward Level Offices** (वडा कार्यालय) - All 6,743 Wards
   - **Ward Offices** in each ward of every local government
   - Ward Chairperson offices and Ward Committee secretariats
   - Local service delivery points for citizens

6. **Specialized Service Offices**
   - **Immigration Offices**: Tribhuvan International Airport, Kakarbhitta, Birgunj, Belahiya/Bhairahawa, Nepalgunj, Rasuwa, etc.
   - **Customs Offices**: All major border points and dry ports
   - **Passport Offices**: Department of Passport offices nationwide
   - **Land Revenue Offices (Malpot)**: One in each district for land registration
   - **Transport Management Offices**: Vehicle registration and licensing
   - **Vital Registration Offices**: Birth, death, marriage registration
   - **Tax Offices**: Inland Revenue Department offices
   - **Border Security Posts**: APF and police border posts
   - **Nagarik App Service Centers**: Citizen service centers

#### Primary Sources to Consult
1. **Official Government Websites**
   - Nepal Government official portal (nepal.gov.np)
   - Office of Prime Minister & Council of Ministers (opmcm.gov.np)
   - Individual ministry websites (all located at Singha Durbar, Kathmandu)
   - Provincial government websites:
     - Koshi Province: koshi.gov.np
     - Madhesh Province: madhesh.gov.np  
     - Bagmati Province: bagamati.gov.np
     - Gandaki Province: gandaki.gov.np
     - Lumbini Province: lumbini.gov.np
     - Karnali Province: karnali.gov.np

2. **Legal Documents**
   - Constitution of Nepal 2015

3. **Administrative Records**
   - Government telephone directories

   - Civil service postings and transfers

4. **Geographic References**
   - Nepal's administrative divisions
   - Electoral constituency maps
   - Census data and administrative boundaries

#### Data Collection Format

For each government office, collect:

**Basic Information:**
- Official name (English and Nepali)
- Office type/category
- Administrative level (Federal/Provincial/District/Local/Ward)
- Parent organization
- Location (Province, District, Municipality/Rural Municipality, Ward if applicable)

**Contact Information:**
- Physical address
- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Website URLs

**Administrative Details:**
- Establishment date
- Legal basis for establishment
- Key functions and responsibilities
- Organizational structure
- Staff strength (if available)

**Geographic Coverage:**
- Jurisdiction area
- Service delivery points
- Branch offices or sub-offices
