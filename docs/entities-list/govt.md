# Nepal Government Structure

This document defines the complete structure of Nepal's government entities. It follows Nepal's federal democratic republic structure established by the 2015 Constitution.

## Federal Level (संघीय तह)

### Executive Branch (कार्यपालिका)

#### Office of the President (राष्ट्रपतिको कार्यालय)
- **Location**: Rashtrapati Bhawan (Sheetal Niwas), Maharajgunj, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Ceremonial Head of State

#### Office of the Vice President (उपराष्ट्रपतिको कार्यालय)
- **Location**: Kantipath, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Deputy Head of State

#### Prime Minister's Office (प्रधानमन्त्रीको कार्यालय)
- **Location**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Head of Government and cabinet coordination
- **Also oversees**: National Planning Commission, National Investigation Department

#### Council of Ministers (मन्त्रिपरिषद्)
- **Location**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Executive decision-making body

#### Federal Ministries (संघीय मन्त्रालयहरू)

##### Core Ministries
- **Ministry of Home Affairs** (गृह मन्त्रालय)
  - **Location**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu
  - **Key Departments**: Immigration, National ID & Civil Registration, Prison Management
  - **Subordinate Forces**: Nepal Police (HQ: Naxal), Armed Police Force (HQ: Halchowk)

- **Ministry of Finance** (अर्थ मन्त्रालय)
  - **Location**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu
  - **Key Departments**: Inland Revenue (Lazimpat), Customs (Tripureshwor), Financial Comptroller General (Anamnagar)

- **Ministry of Foreign Affairs** (परराष्ट्र मन्त्रालय)
  - **Location**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu
  - **Key Departments**: Passport (Tripureshwor), Consular Services

- **Ministry of Defense** (रक्षा मन्त्रालय)
  - **Location**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu
  - **Oversees**: Nepal Army (HQ: Bhadrakali, Kathmandu)

##### Development Ministries
- **Ministry of Federal Affairs and General Administration** (संघीय मामिला तथा सामान्य प्रशासन मन्त्रालय)
  - **Function**: Coordinates with provincial and local governments, manages civil service

- **Ministry of Education, Science and Technology** (शिक्षा, विज्ञान तथा प्रविधि मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Agencies**: Department of Education (Sanothimi), National Examinations Board, University Grants Commission

- **Ministry of Health and Population** (स्वास्थ्य तथा जनसंख्या मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Health Services (Teku), Drug Administration, Nepal Medical Council

- **Ministry of Energy, Water Resources and Irrigation** (ऊर्जा, जलस्रोत तथा सिँचाइ मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Electricity Development, Water Resources & Irrigation, Hydrology & Meteorology

- **Ministry of Physical Infrastructure and Transport** (भौतिक पूर्वाधार तथा यातायात मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Roads (Babarmahal), Transport Management (Minbhawan), Railways

- **Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock Development** (कृषि तथा पशुपंक्षी विकास मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Agriculture (Harihar Bhawan), Livestock Services, Food Technology & Quality Control

- **Ministry of Industry, Commerce and Supplies** (उद्योग, वाणिज्य तथा आपूर्ति मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Industry (Tripureshwor), Mines & Geology (Lainchaur), Company Registrar

- **Ministry of Labour, Employment and Social Security** (श्रम, रोजगार तथा सामाजिक सुरक्षा मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Foreign Employment (Tahachal)

- **Ministry of Forest and Environment** (वन तथा वातावरण मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Forests & Soil Conservation (Babarmahal), National Parks & Wildlife Conservation

- **Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation** (संस्कृति, पर्यटन तथा नागरिक उड्डयन मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Agencies**: Tourism (Bhrikutimandap), Civil Aviation Authority (Babarmahal), Archaeology

- **Ministry of Communications and Information Technology** (सञ्चार तथा सूचना प्रविधि मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Information & Broadcasting, Information Technology, Postal Service (Dilli Bazar)

- **Ministry of Youth and Sports** (युवा तथा खेलकुद मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Agency**: National Sports Council (Tripureshwor)

- **Ministry of Women, Children and Senior Citizens** (महिला, बालबालिका तथा ज्येष्ठ नागरिक मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Agency**: Social Welfare Council (Harihar Bhawan)

- **Ministry of Law, Justice and Parliamentary Affairs** (कानुन, न्याय तथा संसदीय मामिला मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Agency**: Nepal Law Commission (Baneshwor)

- **Ministry of Urban Development** (सहरी विकास मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Department**: Urban Development & Building Construction (Babarmahal)

- **Ministry of Land Management, Cooperatives and Poverty Alleviation** (भूमि व्यवस्था, सहकारी तथा गरिबी निवारण मन्त्रालय)
  - **Key Departments**: Land Management & Archive (Babarmahal), Survey (Minbhawan), Cooperatives

### Legislative Branch (व्यवस्थापिका)

#### Federal Parliament (संघीय संसद्)
- **Location**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu

##### House of Representatives (प्रतिनिधि सभा)
- **Function**: Lower house of Parliament
- **Secretariat**: Located in Singha Durbar

##### National Assembly (राष्ट्रिय सभा)
- **Function**: Upper house of Parliament
- **Secretariat**: Located in Singha Durbar

### Judicial Branch (न्यायपालिका)

#### Supreme Court (सर्वोच्च अदालत)
- **Location**: Ram Shah Path, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Highest court with nationwide jurisdiction

#### High Courts (उच्च अदालत)
- **Total**: 7 High Courts (one per province)
- **Locations**: Biratnagar (Koshi), Janakpur (Madhesh), Patan (Bagmati), Pokhara (Gandaki), Butwal (Lumbini), Surkhet (Karnali), Dipayal (Sudurpashchim)

#### Appellate Courts (पुनरावेदन अदालत)
- **Function**: Intermediate appellate level between District and High Courts

#### District Courts (जिल्ला अदालत)
- **Total**: 77 District Courts (one per district)
- **Function**: First instance courts for civil and criminal cases

#### Special Courts (विशेष अदालतहरू)

##### Administrative Court (प्रशासनिक अदालत)

##### Revenue Tribunal (राजस्व न्यायाधिकरण)

##### Labour Court (श्रम अदालत)

##### Banking Offences Court (बैंकिङ कसुर अदालत)

##### Special Court for Corruption Cases (भ्रष्टाचार मुद्दाका लागि विशेष अदालत)

### Constitutional Bodies (संवैधानिक निकायहरू)

#### Election Commission (निर्वाचन आयोग)
- **Head Office**: Kantipath, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Conducts and oversees all elections
- **District Offices**: 77 District Election Offices

#### Commission for the Investigation of Abuse of Authority (CIAA) (अख्तियार दुरुपयोग अनुसन्धान आयोग)
- **Head Office**: Tangal, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Anti-corruption watchdog
- **Regional Offices**: Pokhara, Surkhet, and other provinces

#### Auditor General (महालेखापरीक्षक)
- **Office**: Babarmahal, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Audits government finances and reports to Parliament

#### Public Service Commission (लोक सेवा आयोग)
- **Central Office**: Anamnagar, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Civil service recruitment and examinations
- **Regional Offices**: One in each province

#### National Human Rights Commission (राष्ट्रिय मानव अधिकार आयोग)
- **Head Office**: Lalitpur (Kathmandu Valley)
- **Function**: Protects and promotes human rights

#### National Information Commission (राष्ट्रिय सूचना आयोग)
- **Office**: Kathmandu
- **Function**: Ensures citizens' right to information

#### National Natural Resources and Fiscal Commission (राष्ट्रिय प्राकृतिक स्रोत तथा वित्त आयोग)
- **Office**: Kathmandu
- **Function**: Revenue sharing formulas between government levels

#### Other Constitutional Commissions
- **National Women Commission** (राष्ट्रिय महिला आयोग)
- **National Dalit Commission** (राष्ट्रिय दलित आयोग)
- **Indigenous Nationalities Commission** (आदिवासी जनजाति आयोग)
- **Madheshi Commission** (मधेशी आयोग)
- **Tharu Commission** (थारु आयोग)
- **Muslim Commission** (मुस्लिम आयोग)
- **National Inclusion Commission** (राष्ट्रिय समावेशी आयोग)

### Federal Departments and Agencies

#### Nepal Police (नेपाल प्रहरी)
- **Headquarters**: Naxal, Kathmandu
- **Structure**: Provincial Police Offices (7), District Police Offices (77), over 2,000 police posts
- **Function**: Primary law enforcement agency

#### Armed Police Force (सशस्त्र प्रहरी बल)
- **Headquarters**: Halchowk, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Paramilitary force for border security, counter-insurgency, disaster response
- **Structure**: Brigade headquarters in each province, battalion-level units

#### Nepal Army (नेपाली सेना)
- **Headquarters**: Bhadrakali, Kathmandu
- **Function**: National defense and disaster response
- **Structure**: Regional divisions across the country

#### National Investigation Department (राष्ट्रिय अनुसन्धान विभाग)
- **Head Office**: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu (under PM's Office)
- **Function**: Intelligence agency for national security
- **Structure**: Branch offices in provinces and major districts

#### Department of Immigration (अध्यागमन विभाग)
- **Head Office**: Kalikasthan/Dillibazar, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Regulates entry/exit of foreigners, issues visas
- **Offices**: Tribhuvan International Airport, major border crossings (Kakarbhitta, Birgunj, Belahiya, Nepalgunj, Rasuwa, etc.)

#### Nepal Rastra Bank (नेपाल राष्ट्र बैंक)
- **Head Office**: Baluwatar, Kathmandu
- **Function**: Central bank, monetary policy regulation
- **Branch Offices**: Provincial centers (Biratnagar, Janakpur, Pokhara, Nepalgunj, etc.)

### The Seven Provinces

#### Koshi Province (कोशी प्रदेश) - Province No. 1
- **Capital**: Biratnagar, Morang District
- **Districts**: 14 districts in eastern Nepal
- **Government Structure**: Governor, Chief Minister, Provincial Assembly, 9 ministries
- **Website**: koshi.gov.np

#### Madhesh Province (मधेश प्रदेश) - Province No. 2
- **Capital**: Janakpurdham, Dhanusha District
- **Districts**: 8 districts in southeastern Terai
- **Government Structure**: Governor, Chief Minister, Provincial Assembly, 12 ministries
- **Website**: madhesh.gov.np

#### Bagmati Province (बागमती प्रदेश) - Province No. 3
- **Capital**: Hetauda, Makwanpur District
- **Districts**: 13 districts including Kathmandu Valley
- **Government Structure**: Governor, Chief Minister, Provincial Assembly, 11 ministries
- **Website**: bagamati.gov.np

#### Gandaki Province (गण्डकी प्रदेश) - Province No. 4
- **Capital**: Pokhara, Kaski District
- **Districts**: 11 districts in central-western Nepal
- **Government Structure**: Governor, Chief Minister, Provincial Assembly, 8 ministries
- **Website**: gandaki.gov.np

#### Lumbini Province (लुम्बिनी प्रदेश) - Province No. 5
- **Capital**: Deukhuri, Dang District
- **Districts**: 12 districts in mid-western Terai and hills
- **Government Structure**: Governor, Chief Minister, Provincial Assembly, 13 ministries
- **Website**: lumbini.gov.np

#### Karnali Province (कर्णाली प्रदेश) - Province No. 6
- **Capital**: Birendranagar, Surkhet District
- **Districts**: 10 districts in northwestern Nepal
- **Government Structure**: Governor, Chief Minister, Provincial Assembly, 8 ministries
- **Website**: karnali.gov.np

#### Sudurpashchim Province (सुदूरपश्चिम प्रदेश) - Province No. 7
- **Capital**: Godawari, Kailali District (offices initially in Dhangadhi)
- **Districts**: 9 districts in far-western Nepal
- **Government Structure**: Governor, Chief Minister, Provincial Assembly, 7 ministries
- **Website**: sudurpashchim.gov.np
