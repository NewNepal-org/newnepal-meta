### Case Title
Corruption Case Regarding the Construction of a Bridge without Road Access in Gorkha

**Date of Initiation:** 2023-02-26 (Date of filing case by CIAA)

**Case Status:** Under Investigation / In Court

#### **Description**
<p>The Commission for the Investigation of Abuse of Authority (CIAA) has filed a corruption case against three individuals, including the former Physical Infrastructure Secretary of Gandaki Province, Ishworchand Marahatta. The case pertains to the construction of a concrete bridge over the Daraudi River in Gorkha, in a location where there is no road access. The bridge was intended to connect Jarebar of Gorkha Municipality-3 and Siranchok Rural Municipality-5.</p><p>The investigation was initiated after media reports and subsequent public outcry. It is alleged that the then Minister for Physical Infrastructure Development of Gandaki Province, Ramsharan Basnet, misused his authority to have the bridge constructed near his personal farm. The total cost of the bridge is reported to be around Rs 53.9 million.</p><p>The CIAA alleges that the accused colluded to change the location and name of the bridge from what was originally agreed upon, thereby abusing their authority and causing a significant financial loss to the state by investing public funds in an inappropriate project.</p>

#### **Main Allegations**
<ul>
    <li>Construction of a public bridge without connecting roads.</li>
    <li>Misuse of public authority for personal benefit.</li>
    <li>Unauthorized change of project location and name in violation of the original agreement.</li>
    <li>Causing a financial loss to the Government of Nepal amounting to <strong>NPR 54,552,671</strong>.</li>
    <li>Spending budget without obtaining necessary permissions, such as from the forest department.</li>
</ul>

#### **Financial Details**
*   **Total Alleged Corruption:** NPR 54,552,671
*   **Total Project Cost:** Approximately NPR 53.9 million
*   **Amount allegedly embezzled by Ram Kumar Shrestha:** NPR 571,780 (for naming an imaginary road)

---
### **Individuals and Entities Involved**

#### **Accused Individuals**
1.  **Ishworchand Marahatta (इश्वरचन्द मरहट्टा)**
    *   **Role:** Then-Secretary, Ministry of Physical Infrastructure, Gandaki Province.
    *   **Allegation:** Main accused. The CIAA has filed a case demanding recovery of the full loss amount (NPR 54,552,671) from him, along with a fine and imprisonment. He is accused of abusing his position by approving the expenditure of state funds in an inappropriate location.

2.  **Ram Kumar Shrestha (रामकुमार श्रेष्ठ)**
    *   **Role:** Then-Acting Director, State Road Directorate, Gandaki Province.
    *   **Allegation:** Accused of embezzling NPR 571,780, which was paid for the process of naming a non-existent road related to the bridge project.

3.  **Omraj Dhungana (ओमराज ढुंगाना)**
    *   **Role:** Senior Division Engineer, Infrastructure Development Office, Gorkha.
    *   **Allegation:** Accused of spending the project budget without first obtaining the required permission from the forest authorities.

#### **Persons of Interest**
1.  **Ramsharan Basnet**
    *   **Role:** Then-Minister for Physical Infrastructure Development, Gandaki Province.
    *   **Connection:** Criticized for allegedly using his power and authority to plan and construct the bridge near his personal house and farm. The CIAA investigation began after media reports highlighted his role.

#### **Investigating Agencies**
*   **Commission for the Investigation of Abuse of Authority (CIAA)**, Central Office.

---
### **Timeline of Events**

*   **August 23, 2022:** The CIAA confirmed it had started an investigation into the bridge's construction. The agency's team had already conducted a field examination and began collecting documents from the Infrastructure Development Office in Gorkha and the provincial ministry.
*   **February 26, 2023:** The CIAA filed a formal corruption case at the Special Court against Ishworchand Marahatta, Ram Kumar Shrestha, and Omraj Dhungana.
