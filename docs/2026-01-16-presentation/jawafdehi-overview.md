## What is jawafdehi.org?

An open-source civic technology platform dedicated to promoting transparency and accountability in Nepali governance.

**Mission**: Create a comprehensive, transparent, and accessible database of corruption cases in Nepal, empowering citizens with information and promoting accountability in governance.

---

## Its value proposition - What would be most useful?

- **For Citizens**: Access to verified corruption case information
- **For Journalists**: Structured data and research support for investigations
- **For Researchers**: Comprehensive datasets for academic analysis
- **For Civil Society**: Evidence-based advocacy tools
- **For Nepal**: Long-term institutional memory against corruption
